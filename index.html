WELCOME TO THE HOME OF TRINBARJAM

Congratulations to the People's Democratic Revolution Party for once again winning the general election for the second year in a row. We would also like to congratulate the United Progressive Movement as well as all the other political parties for their great work even though they fell short of victory. Additionally, we would like to point out that we went through the election without any conflict. We thank you all for making the campaigns safe, peaceful and enjoyable. We at Trinbarjam are looking forward to next year's election and we hope it will be even better than the election conducted this year.
